Common Questions

A few of the questions clients usually ask before placing an order.

Can I send artwork later?

Yes. You can start with the product requirement and share artwork once size, stock, and finishing have been finalized.

Do you handle urgent jobs?

Yes, depending on quantity and finishing requirements. Mention your deadline in the form so we can advise accurately.

Can I request samples?

For many jobs, yes. Sample availability depends on the product category and whether the finish is standard or custom.
