Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Data We Collect

We collect personal information when you place an order, create an account, or contact us. This includes:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Order details and preferences
  • Payment information (processed securely)
  • Communication history

How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders
  • Provide customer support
  • Send order updates and confirmations
  • Comply with legal and financial obligations
  • Improve our services (with your consent)

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, and disclosure. All transactions are encrypted using SSL technology.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only with trusted partners who assist in delivering our services (e.g., logistics providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at privacy@zakprinting.com.
