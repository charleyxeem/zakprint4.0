Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, order process, and printing solutions.

What file formats do you accept? expand_more

We accept PDF, AI, EPS, and high-res PNG/JPG files. PDF is preferred as it ensures consistent color and layout reproduction. For vector artwork, AI and EPS formats with embedded fonts are ideal.

What is your standard turnaround time? expand_more

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days. Express options are available for 1-2 business day delivery. Urgent orders should be discussed directly with our team for availability and feasibility.

Do you offer bulk discounts? expand_more

Yes! We offer volume discounts for orders above certain quantities. Contact us with your order details for a custom quote tailored to your needs.

Do you provide color proofs before printing? expand_more

For large orders, we provide digital proofs for review and approval before production. Physical proofs can be arranged for premium orders on request.

What printing methods do you offer? expand_more

We offer both digital and offset printing. Digital is ideal for small runs and quick turnarounds. Offset printing provides superior quality and cost-efficiency for large volumes.
