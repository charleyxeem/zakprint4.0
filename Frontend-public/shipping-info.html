Shipping & Delivery

Everything you need to know about how we get your order to you.

Delivery Timelines

Standard Delivery

3-5 business days

Our regular turnaround time for most orders. Suitable for planned projects and regular inventory needs.

Express Delivery

1-2 business days

Urgent orders with faster production. Premium charges apply. Limited availability.

Delivery Zones & Charges

We deliver across Pakistan with delivery charges based on location and order weight:

Karachi & Lahore: Free shipping on orders above PKR 5,000

Islamabad, Rawalpindi: PKR 300-500 based on order size

Other Cities: PKR 500-1,500 depending on location

Same-Day Delivery: Available in major cities with additional charges
